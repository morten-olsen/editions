## Switch

<div style={{ color: "var(--color-ink-secondary)", fontSize: "0.833rem", marginBottom: "2rem", maxWidth: "65ch" }}>
  For binary settings with immediate effect. The label sits left, the switch right — a natural reading order for "Enable this" patterns. The thumb slides with a gentle ease, and the track fills with accent when active.
</div>

## Separator

<div style={{ color: "var(--color-ink-secondary)", fontSize: "0.833rem", marginBottom: "2rem", maxWidth: "65ch" }}>
