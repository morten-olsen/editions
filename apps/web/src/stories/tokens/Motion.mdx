# Motion

<div style={{ fontSize: "1.2rem", color: "var(--color-ink-secondary)", lineHeight: 1.625, marginTop: "1rem", marginBottom: "4rem", maxWidth: "65ch" }}>
  Motion in Editions should feel like turning a page — smooth, purposeful, and invisible when done right. Transitions exist to maintain spatial orientation, not to delight or distract.
</div>

<div style={{ height: "1px", background: "var(--color-border)", marginBottom: "4rem" }} />

## Easing

<div style={{ color: "var(--color-ink-secondary)", fontSize: "0.833rem", marginBottom: "2.5rem", maxWidth: "65ch" }}>
  Two curves. Gentle is the default — a standard ease that feels natural and unremarkable. Out-soft is for elements entering the viewport — a soft deceleration that feels like settling into place.
</div>

<EasingCurves />

<div style={{ height: "1px", background: "var(--color-border)", margin: "4rem 0" }} />

## Duration

<div style={{ color: "var(--color-ink-secondary)", fontSize: "0.833rem", marginBottom: "2.5rem", maxWidth: "65ch" }}>
  Shorter durations for small, frequent interactions. Longer for larger transitions. Nothing exceeds 500ms — the reader should never wait for the interface.
</div>
